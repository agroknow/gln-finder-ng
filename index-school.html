The Meatrix Interactive 360

The Meatrix Interactive 360 feature is a great way to educate yourself, friends and family (and students!) about factory farming. Roll over the objects on the farm and click them to get in depth information about each subject.

Farmerama

This is an online game, which requires the user to be responsible for developing, expanding and taking care of a farm. The user has to take care of several aspects, such as to feed the farm animals, water the plants and construct new buildings.The interface of the game is multilingual and already translated in 31 languages.
